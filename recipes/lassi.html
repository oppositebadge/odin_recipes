Lassi

Photo by Gaurav Dhwaj Khadka

Description

Wonderful accompaniment for a spicy Indian meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chopped cilantro leaves
  • 2/3 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pinch black pepper

Steps

  1. Combine the yogurt, water, milk, cumin, cilantro, salt, and pepper in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve plain or over ice.
