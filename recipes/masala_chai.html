Masala Chai

Photo by aesop

Description

This chai is hot and flavorful thanks to its use of cardamom, clove, and peppercorn â€” gotta love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 + 1/2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 whole cardamom pod
  • 1 whole clove
  • 2 black peppercorns
  • 3 teaspoons black tea leaves
  • 1/2 cup warm milk

Steps

  1. Combine the water and sugar in a small saucepan.
  2. Bring the water to boil.
  3. Add the cardamom pod, clove, peppercorns and tea leaves.
  4. Remove from heat, and let the mixture steep for 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Strain into a cup and add milk
